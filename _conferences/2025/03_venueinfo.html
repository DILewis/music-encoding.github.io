Venue information

The conference will take place at City St George's, University of London, Northampton Square, EC1V 0HB.

We will be using various rooms in several buildings of the campus, but all of them connect, so we recommend you always use the main entrance on the square itself. The conference registration will be in the atrium.

Teas and coffees will be provided on Tuesday-Friday, and vegetarian and vegan sandwich lunch on Wednesday and Thursday. For other days, there is a canteen on the first floor, but also many nearby lunch options including a large variety of places in Exmouth Market.

Finding your rooms

Helpers and organisers will be on hand to get you to your rooms, and there is also a dedicated navigation app called CityNav. Below is a list of conference rooms and how to get there from the big staircases in the main entrance (near the big 'CITY' sign).

Monday

B104 & B103

Go one flight up the main staircase in the entrance area to the first floor. Turn right and head down the corridor. The Rooms B103 and B104 are on your right.

Tuesday

C309

Go one flight up the main staircase in the entrance area to the first floor. Turn left and head down the corridor, following the line labelled 'Tait building'. When you reach a wide area with a chess set, head up the stairs that are on your right. You need to go up two more floors.

ELG04

Go one flight down from the entrance area to the lower floor. The room is well-signed and on through the doors on your left.

B200

Go two flights up the main staircase. B200 is on the landing behind big double doors.

Pavillion

This is in the main entrance area.

Wednesday

B200

Go two flights up the main staircase. B200 is on the landing behind big double doors.

A309

This is probably the longest walk. Go up one floor from the main entrance and turn right, following the line on the floor labelled College Building. It should take you onto a veranda overlooking the square and then into another building. Go two floors up the spiral staircase (there is a lift as well), and turn left. Eventually, you will find A309 on your right.

Thursday

B200

Go two flights up the main staircase. B200 is on the landing behind big double doors.

Friday

B200

Go two flights up the main staircase. B200 is on the landing behind big double doors.

ELG09 and ELG14

Go two flights down the staircase in the main atrium. Head left down the broad corridor. Your room is on this corridor.

Useful spaces

The main entrance area has a variety of seats and desks, along with a cafe, for working and ad hoc meetings. More space is also available two floors below, in the area around the ELG rooms.

Any questions? Contact Organizing Committee Chair, David Lewis, or Programme Committee Chair, Anna Plaksin, at conference2025 at music-encoding.org.

Music Encoding Conference 2025 jointly organised by Goldsmiths University of London and City University of London on behalf of the Music Encoding Initiative community.
